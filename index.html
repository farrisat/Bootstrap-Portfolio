About Me

Born in Saudi Arabia to two insane, American expats who decided to stick around for the Gulf War. Fun family photo of everyone in gas masks (my sister and I were ages 3 and 5, respectively). Moved back to New Mexico, where I had to explain to visiting classmates, that there is indeed a camel skull in my living room. For high school I got the unique experience of actually attending a military boarding school. Have completed my one and done on a full 26.2mi marathon. I have a cat, her name is Toothless. She really has no teeth, I just couldn't pass up on the literal naming.
